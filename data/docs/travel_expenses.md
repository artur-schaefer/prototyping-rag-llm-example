# Travel & Expense Guidelines

**1. Booking Travel**  
Business trips must be booked through the approved travel portal. Economy class is standard for flights under six hours.

**2. Accommodation**  
Hotel expenses are reimbursed up to 120 EUR per night, including taxes.

**3. Meals**  
Per-diem allowances follow local regulations. Receipts must be uploaded within ten days after the trip.

**4. Reimbursement Process**  
Expenses are submitted via the HR portal and typically reimbursed within 14 days.

# Vacation Policy

**1. Annual Leave Entitlement**  
Employees receive 25 days of paid vacation per year. Leave is accrued monthly during the first year.

**2. Request Procedure**  
Vacation requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least two weeks in advance. Approval is subject to team workload and staffing requirements.

**3. Carryover Rules**  
Up to 5 unused days may be carried over into the next calendar year and must be used by March 31.

**4. Special Leave**  
Special leave may be granted for personal events such as marriage, relocation, or family emergencies.

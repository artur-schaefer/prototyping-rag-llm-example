# Remote Work Policy

**1. Eligibility**  
Employees may work remotely for up to three days per week, subject to manager approval.

**2. Equipment**  
Schaefer Corporation provides a laptop and optional monitor. Employees are responsible for a stable internet connection.

**3. Working From Abroad**  
Remote work from abroad is permitted for up to 20 days per year. All data-protection rules remain fully applicable.

**4. Availability**  
During working hours, employees must remain reachable via Slack and email.
